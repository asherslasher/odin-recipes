Eerie Witch Finger Cookies!

Prep time 20 mins

Total time 40 mins

I cannot think of anything more weird and delicious than these witch fingers!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of softened unsalted butter
  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp of almond extract
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 & 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of salt, though use 1/2 tsp if you have salted butter
  • 3/4 cup of whole blanched almonds
  • A few drops of green food colouring
  • 1/4 cup of any red jam (strawberry, raspbelly, lingonberry)

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325Â° degrees.
  2. In a bowl, beat together butter, sugar, egg, almond and vanilla extract. Next, beat in flour, baking powder and salt. Add a few drops of green food coloring until it reaches your desired green. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Next, roll heaping tablespoons of dough into finger shapes for each cookie.
  4. Press almond firmly into one end for the nail. Squeeze in center to create knuckle shape. Using a paring knife or toothpick, make several places to form knuckle lines.
  5. Place cookies on lightly greased baking sheets or parchment lined baking sheets.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until pale golden. Let cool for 3 minutes
  7. On each cookie, lift the almond and add some jam onto nail bed. Press almond back on till jam oozes out from underneath. Let cool.
  8. Makes about 28 large cigar size cookies. If you only use 1 teaspoon of dough, you will get about 4 dozen cookies.
