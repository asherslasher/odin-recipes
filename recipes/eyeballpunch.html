Eyeball Punch!

Prep time 10 mins

Total time 10 mins

*Can be served with or without alcohol!*

Double, double, toil and trouble! Fire burn and cauldron bubble! Bewitching, fruity beverage. Do you dare to taste this witch brew?

Ingredients:

Punch:

  • 4 cups (1 quart) of 100% pineapple juice
  • 4 cups (1 quart) of 100% blood orange juice
  • 3 cups club soda
  • 1/3 cup of 100% lime juice
  • 3 cups of white rum - omit if alcohol free

Eyeballs:

  • 2 cans of lychee fruit in syrup
  • About 40 blueberries

Directions:

  1. First, we need to make the eyeballs. Drain the lychee can and place blueberries in the middle of each lychee.
  2. For extra creepy effect, place two lychee's on a skewer.
  3. Keep the lychee and blueberry eyeballs in the freezer until ready to serve the beverage
  4. Shortly before serving, mix all the liquids together. Keep chilled until serving time and add ice.
