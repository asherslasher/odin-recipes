Spooky Bark!

10 mins

Quick and easy, this is a family tradition to make use of all of that leftover Halloween.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of almond bark or white chocolate melts
  • 6 sandwich cookies broken up into large chunks
  • 1/2 cup of chopped up candy bar pieces (I use Snickers!)
  • 1 cup of candy corn
  • 20-30 candy eyeballs
  • 1 cup broken up pretzel pieces
  • 2-3 tablespoons of various Halloween sprinkles
  • Whatever else you want to use up, though I'd avoid hard candies because of how they melt

Directions:

  1. First, you're going to want to line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. Next, melt your almond bark/chocolate either double broiler method, or in the microwave in 20 second intervals.
  3. Be sure to stir your chocolate often to prevent burning.
  4. Spread a layer of your cookie, pretzel, sprinkle, candy bar mixture on the parchment paper
  5. Drizzle some of the melted chocolate over the mixture. Use a spatula to spread over evenly.
  6. Quickly add the remainder of your candy mixture.
  7. Place in refrigerator until set. This may take an hour.
  8. Once solid, break up into small pieces.
  9. Devour, little monsters!
